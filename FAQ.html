Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EchoQR setup, plans, and features.

What is EchoQR?

EchoQR is an ultra-luxury feedback platform using QR codes to capture instant guest insights without apps.

Do I need special equipment?

Nope. Just print and place our QR codes. Guests scan using their smartphone cameras and web browsers.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Every Starter plan includes a 7-day free trial—no contract, cancel anytime.

How secure is my data?

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, using enterprise-grade security protocols.

Can I customize the feedback forms?

Absolutely—your forms can match your brand colors, questions, and style.

How long does setup take?

Once you submit your onboarding, you'll be live within 24-48 hours!

Can I receive alerts via SMS?

Yes. EchoQR Assist supports real-time SMS and email alerts for sit-down restaurants.

Is EchoQR good for multi-location brands?

Definitely! You can manage all locations under one dashboard.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes—upgrade or downgrade at any time through your manager dashboard.

Does EchoQR help increase positive reviews?

Yes! EchoQR Growth and Elite plans include a Review Booster system to direct happy customers to public sites.

Do you integrate with existing POS systems?

We offer integrations with many leading POS and CRM providers—ask us!

How does billing work?

Billing is monthly via Venmo. No long-term contracts unless requested.

What industries use EchoQR?

Restaurants, hotels, salons, retail, gyms—any brand that prioritizes customer experience.

What happens if I cancel?

You retain access until the end of your billing cycle—no hidden penalties.
