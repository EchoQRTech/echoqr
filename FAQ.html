Frequently Asked Questions

What is EchoQR?

EchoQR is an ultra-luxury feedback platform using QR codes to capture instant guest insights without apps.

Do I need special equipment?

No. Just print and place our QR codes—guests scan using their smartphone cameras and web browsers.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Every Starter plan includes a 7-day free trial—no contract, cancel anytime.

How secure is my data?

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, using enterprise-grade security protocols.

Can I customize the feedback forms?

Absolutely—your forms can match your brand colors, questions, and style.

How long does setup take?

Once you submit onboarding, you’ll be live within 24-48 hours!

Can I receive alerts via SMS?

Yes. EchoQR Assist supports real-time SMS and email alerts for sit-down restaurants.

Is EchoQR good for multi-location brands?

Definitely! You can manage all locations under one dashboard.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes—upgrade or downgrade at any time through your dashboard.

Does EchoQR help increase positive reviews?

Yes! Our Review Booster system helps guide happy customers to public platforms like Google and Yelp.
